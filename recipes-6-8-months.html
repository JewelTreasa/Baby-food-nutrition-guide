Recipes for 6-8 Months

Simple, nutritious purees and mashed foods for your baby's first solid foods journey

Sweet Potato Puree

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium sweet potato
  • Breast milk or formula (for consistency)

Instructions:

  1. Peel and cut sweet potato into small cubes
  2. Steam until very tender (about 15 minutes)
  3. Blend until smooth
  4. Add milk/formula to achieve desired consistency

Tips:

Start with a thinner consistency and gradually thicken as baby adjusts to solid foods.

Apple and Pear Puree

Ingredients:

  • 1 apple, peeled and cored
  • 1 ripe pear, peeled and cored
  • Water for steaming

Instructions:

  1. Cut fruits into small pieces
  2. Steam until soft (about 8-10 minutes)
  3. Blend until smooth
  4. Cool before serving

Tips:

Choose ripe, soft fruits for the smoothest puree. Can be frozen in ice cube trays for later use.

Basic Rice Cereal

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons iron-fortified rice cereal
  • 4-5 tablespoons breast milk or formula

Instructions:

  1. Place cereal in a small bowl
  2. Gradually stir in warm milk/formula
  3. Mix until smooth and runny

Tips:

Start with a very thin consistency (almost liquid) and gradually thicken over time.

Banana Avocado Mash

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 ripe avocado
  • Breast milk or formula (optional, for consistency)

Instructions:

  1. Peel and mash the banana
  2. Scoop out and mash the avocado
  3. Combine both ingredients and mix well
  4. Add milk/formula if needed for smoother consistency

Tips:

Use very ripe bananas and fresh avocados. Best served immediately to prevent browning.

Carrot and Green Bean Puree

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium carrots
  • 1 cup fresh green beans
  • Water for steaming
  • Breast milk or formula (for consistency)

Instructions:

  1. Peel and chop carrots into small pieces
  2. Trim and cut green beans
  3. Steam vegetables until very tender (about 12-15 minutes)
  4. Blend until smooth, adding liquid as needed

Tips:

Can be frozen in small portions. Thaw overnight in refrigerator when needed.

Butternut Squash Puree

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butternut squash, peeled and cubed
  • Water for steaming
  • Breast milk or formula (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Peel and cut squash into small cubes
  2. Steam until very soft (about 15-20 minutes)
  3. Let cool slightly
  4. Blend until smooth, adding liquid if needed

Tips:

Rich in vitamin A and naturally sweet. Perfect for baby's first foods.
