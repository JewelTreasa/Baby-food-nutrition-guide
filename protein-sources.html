Protein Sources

Building blocks for growth and muscle development

Why Protein is Important

Protein is essential for your baby's development as it:

  • Builds and repairs tissues
  • Supports muscle development
  • Produces enzymes and hormones
  • Strengthens the immune system

Best Protein Sources

  • Lean meats (chicken, turkey, beef)
  • Fish (salmon, cod, haddock)
  • Eggs
  • Greek yogurt
  • Beans and lentils
  • Tofu and tempeh

Preparation Tips

  • Start with pureed or finely chopped options
  • Ensure meats are well-cooked
  • Remove all bones and skin
  • Introduce new proteins gradually
  • Watch for any allergic reactions

Age-Appropriate Serving Sizes

  • 6-8 months: 1-2 tablespoons pureed protein
  • 8-10 months: 2-3 tablespoons chopped protein
  • 10-12 months: 3-4 tablespoons chopped protein
  • 12+ months: 4+ tablespoons chopped protein
