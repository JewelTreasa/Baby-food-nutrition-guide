Feeding Guide by Age

6-8 Months

Start with single-ingredient purees and mashed foods

  • Iron-fortified cereals
  • Pureed vegetables
  • Mashed fruits

9-12 Months

Introduce soft finger foods and combinations

  • Soft cooked vegetables
  • Small pieces of fruit
  • Protein-rich foods

12-18 Months

Expand to more complex textures

  • Family foods (cut small)
  • Whole grains
  • Dairy products

18-24 Months

Transition to regular family meals

  • Varied textures
  • All food groups
  • Self-feeding skills
