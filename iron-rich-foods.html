Iron-Rich Foods

Essential for brain development and preventing anemia

Why Iron is Important

Iron is crucial for your baby's development as it:

  • Supports brain development and cognitive function
  • Helps prevent anemia
  • Strengthens the immune system
  • Promotes healthy blood circulation

Best Iron-Rich Foods

  • Lean meats (beef, lamb, pork)
  • Iron-fortified cereals
  • Dark leafy greens (spinach, kale)
  • Beans and lentils
  • Eggs
  • Fish (especially salmon)

Tips for Iron Absorption

  • Pair iron-rich foods with vitamin C sources
  • Cook in cast iron pans
  • Include both heme and non-heme iron sources
  • Avoid serving with calcium-rich foods at the same meal

Age-Appropriate Serving Sizes

  • 6-8 months: 1-2 tablespoons pureed meat
  • 8-10 months: 2-3 tablespoons chopped meat
  • 10-12 months: 3-4 tablespoons chopped meat
  • 12+ months: 4+ tablespoons chopped meat
